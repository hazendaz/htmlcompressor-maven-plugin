htmlcompressor:html

Full name:

com.github.hazendaz.maven:htmlcompressor-maven-plugin:1.9.4:html

Description:

Compress HTML files.

Attributes:

  • The goal is thread-safe and supports parallel builds.
  • Binds by default to the lifecycle phase: compile.
